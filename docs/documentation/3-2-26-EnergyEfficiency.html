3-2-26 Energy Efficiency¶

Anthony Ge, Mason Tsai, Zachary Martinez, Anthony Wheeler

Goals¶

  • Test intake and conveyor for energy efficiency

Results¶

  • Tested voltage at different intake speeds.

  • Tested voltage of counterrollers.

  • Tested voltage at different conveyor speeds.

    • We found that the conveyor used a lot of amperage (40 A at 4 V)

  • Further loosened the counterrollers

  • Removed bar in the middle of the conveyor

    • Still used a lot of power

  • Placed a pulley (not attached to anything) between the belts on the conveyor.

  • Tested shooter at various voltages.

  • Discussed various improvements with other subteams

  • Found new voltages for intake

Continuation¶

  • Improve design
